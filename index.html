About

I'm Ben Neilson, founder of Craft QA. I work with software teams that need stronger quality practices, better release confidence, and a more practical approach to testing. My focus is on helping teams reduce risk, improve coverage, and deliver reliable software without slowing down delivery.

Experience & Expertise

With 13 years in QA, including work with organizations such as EA Games and Telus, I bring hands-on experience across test strategy, automation, performance testing, and quality leadership. I help teams build testing approaches that fit their product, workflow, and release goals rather than forcing a one-size-fits-all process.

That includes improving regression coverage, introducing or refining automation, strengthening release readiness, and giving teams clearer visibility into quality risks before they become production issues.

How I Work With Clients

  • 1. Assess

    • Review your product, workflow, risks, and current testing approach.

    • Identify the biggest quality gaps affecting releases, speed, or confidence.

  • 2. Build the Plan

    • Create a practical QA roadmap covering automation, manual coverage, performance, and process improvements.

    • Prioritize the work that will deliver the fastest and highest-value impact.

  • 3. Deliver and Improve

    • Implement the agreed testing approach, measure results, and refine over time.

    • Leave your team with stronger quality processes, clearer visibility, and more reliable releases.
